2022 Edition

The second edition of the Festival is built from the recurring theme of Disequilibrium. Be it the mess inside our heads, in our lives, in our world, or in the Universe, disequilibrium appears everywhere and in every moment.
We will be waiting for you to find our reciprocal equilibriums, together!

What does Paneurethic mean?

Paneurethic is a neologism we created to synthetize the meaning and values of Scambi. It derives from the Greek pan (all), eurisko (discover) and ethos (ethical living). We wish to combine diverse realities that would never meet in their original contexts, while celebrating and respecting our differences, discovering and developing inspiring ideas.

The Labs

Labs are paneurethic experiences where participants are stimulated to cooperate and interact in order to acknowledge and understand what appears as strangea and odd, but also to know better themselves, others and the world around them.

The Pinoli

Pinoli are not just the sweet seeds that make pesto so delicious; when it comes to Scambi, they are events where you can meet new people: book presentations, exhibitions of innovative technological solutions, exchanges of secondhand clothing, and much more.

Dissolvenze

Dissolvenze is our section dedicated to cinema, and it also our short movie contest gathering works from all over the world. Each year, it follows the main theme of the Scambi edition.
